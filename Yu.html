Young Understanding

"Young Understanding", "Y.U.", or mostly refered to as "Yu" or "Yuki" is a FrozenEmber programme focusing on artificial intelligence. The main goals of Young Understanding, ordered by importance are:

1. Speed of learning

2. Machine storage size

3. The "human feel" inside of machines

4. Creative thinking
